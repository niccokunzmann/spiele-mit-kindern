To make a word or sentence italic, surround it with a * or _ symbol. Only this *word* and this _word_ should be italic. And these *two words* should be italic, as well as _these two_. To make a word or sentence bold, surround it with ** or __ symbols. Only this **word** and this __word__ should be bold. And these **two words** should be bold, as well as __these two__.
